# Dodona

Research into symbolic cognitive reasoning applied to institutional
decision-making. This repository contains papers, sealed forecast
documents, and post-resolution analyses.

## Forecast Engineering

Dodona is a symbolic reasoning framework for institutional actor
modeling and multi-horizon forecasting. Unlike statistical or
LLM-based approaches, Dodona produces auditable reasoning chains
with full evidence provenance, calibrated confidence scores, and
cryptographically sealed forecasts committed before each decision.

Every prediction in this repository was sealed before the relevant
decision date, blockchain-timestamped, and published publicly.
Decryption keys are released after resolution. No trust required
-- anyone can verify independently.

## Contents

Papers and technical reports will appear here as they are published.
Sealed forecast documents and post-resolution analyses are organized
by institution and decision date.
